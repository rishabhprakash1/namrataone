Who We Are

Serving Society With Compassion & Integrity

Bharat Vikas Parishad (BVP) is a national-level socio-cultural voluntary organization motivated by the ideals of Swami Vivekananda. We strive towards the physical, intellectual, moral, and spiritual growth of citizens.

The Samarpan Shakha, located in Mira Road, actively undertakes projects across various domains. Our primary focus is on uplifting the underprivileged through education, moral studies, essential distributions, and fostering a deep sense of national pride.

Our Core Principles

The 5 Pillars of BVP

Guiding our daily actions and our vision for a beautifully developed nation.

Sampark

(Fellowship)

Establishing contact and building a network of dedicated individuals who are willing to serve the nation.

Sahyog

(Cooperation)

Working together in harmony, bringing resources and efforts to achieve our common socio-cultural goals.

Sanskar

(Moral Orientation)

Inculcating moral, cultural, and spiritual values in the younger generation for a profound national character.

Sewa

(Service)

Selfless duty towards the underprivileged. Providing resources, education, and medical aid to those in need.

Samarpan

(Commitment)

Total dedication and unwavering commitment to the motherland and to the cause of humanity.
